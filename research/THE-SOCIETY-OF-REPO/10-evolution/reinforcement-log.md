# Reinforcement Log

This log records K-line, frame, analogy, and loop-level reinforcement or weakening events.

## Format

```markdown
## {date} — {artifact-id} — {reinforce | weaken}

**Settlement:** {settlement-id}
**Outcome:** {outcome summary}
**Credit assignment:** {which loop components helped or harmed}
**Action:** {reinforced | weakened}
**New count:** reinforcement_count={N}, weakening_count={N}
**Notes:** {optional notes}
```
