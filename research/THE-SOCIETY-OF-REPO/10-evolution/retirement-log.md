# Retirement Log

This log records agency retirement, merge, and successor-lineage events.

## Format

```markdown
## {date} — {agency-id} — {Retired | Merged | Replaced}

**Agency:** {agency name}
**Reason:** {retirement or merge reason}
**Performance summary:** {brief summary}
**Lineage:** {specialized-from / merged-into links}
**Decision:** {settlement-id}
```
