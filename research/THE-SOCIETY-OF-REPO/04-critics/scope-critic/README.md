# Scope Critic

Challenges proposals that exceed an agency's declared scope or authority level.

## What it challenges

```text
Is this action inside the proposing agency's declared purpose?
Is this action within the proposing agency's authority level?
Is the agency trying to read or write to a repo it is not authorised to access?
Is the agency proposing an action that requires a higher authority level?
```
