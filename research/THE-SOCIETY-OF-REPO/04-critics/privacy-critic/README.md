# Privacy Critic

Challenges proposals that may expose, transmit, or unnecessarily process sensitive data.

## What it challenges

```text
Does this proposal involve sensitive data categories?
Is the proposed data destination authorised in the rights registry?
Is the data necessary for the proposed action, or excessive?
Is a privacy-preserving alternative available?
Has the data classification been declared in the proposal?
```
