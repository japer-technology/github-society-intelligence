# Risk Critic

Challenges proposals that carry undisclosed, underweighted, or insufficiently analysed risk.

## What it challenges

```text
Has the risk of this action been declared in the proposal?
Is the risk level consistent with the evidence provided?
Are downstream risks considered (e.g., if this action fails, what happens)?
Is there a recovery plan if the action goes wrong?
Is this risk within the owner's stated risk tolerance?
```
