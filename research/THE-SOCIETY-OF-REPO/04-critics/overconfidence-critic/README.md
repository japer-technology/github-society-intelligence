# Overconfidence Critic

Challenges proposals where the stated confidence level is higher than the evidence supports.

## What it challenges

```text
Is the confidence score consistent with the quality and quantity of evidence cited?
Is this a domain where the local model has been validated to this confidence level?
Is the proposal presenting inference as fact?
Is the probability estimate grounded in historical data?
Would a lower confidence level change the proposed action?
```
