# Evidence Critic

Challenges proposals that make claims without sufficient supporting evidence or reasoning trace quality.

## What it challenges

```text
Is there a citation for this claim?
Is the evidence source reliable?
Is the confidence level consistent with the evidence quality?
Was the evidence actually read, or only inferred?
Is the evidence current?
Does the proposal explain its method?
Are alternatives considered and rejected visibly?
Are observability limits and opaque-model dependencies disclosed?
```
