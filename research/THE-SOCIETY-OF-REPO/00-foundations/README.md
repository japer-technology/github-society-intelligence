## Upstream theoretical archive

The foundations in this folder are engineered against a separate,
research-grade archive of the source material:
[`../../THE-SOCIETY-OF-MIND/`](../../THE-SOCIETY-OF-MIND/README.md).

That archive carries the full text of Marvin Minsky's *The Society of
Mind* (Simon & Schuster, 1986), a twelve-file annotated companion, a
per-section implementation analysis for every section of the book, and
the secondary literature (the 1986 *Whole Earth Review* excerpts, the
1988 ONR Final Report, and Mikkilineni & Michaels, 2025). The
authoritative term-by-term map between Minsky's vocabulary and SOR —
including the nine deliberate divergences D1–D9 — lives in
[`../../THE-SOCIETY-OF-MIND/12-crosswalk-to-society-of-repo.md`](../../THE-SOCIETY-OF-MIND/12-crosswalk-to-society-of-repo.md).

When a foundations document below cites "Minsky 1986", "Minsky 1988",
or "2025 Society of Minds", the linked entries in
[07-research-crosswalk.md](07-research-crosswalk.md) point to the
specific files in the upstream archive.
