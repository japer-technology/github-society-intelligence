## What the image shows

A small line drawing of three blocks: two upright cubes standing
parallel with a clear gap between them, and a longer rectangular
block laid horizontally across their tops. A caption reads,
*Two standing blocks and a lying block.*

## What it illustrates

The first instance of the structure the child is trying to learn —
an arch wide enough to push a toy car through. The figure introduces
the running example for the chapter: how a learner moves from a
single experience to a general concept by collecting, comparing, and
revising candidate descriptions.
