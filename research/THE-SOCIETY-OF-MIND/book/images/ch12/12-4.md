## What the image shows

Two upright blocks set apart, with a triangular wedge resting across
them as the top. The caption reads, *Two standing blocks and
something else. The standing blocks must not touch. They must
support the other thing. The other thing may be a WEDGE OR A BLOCK.*

## What it illustrates

A positive instance that the previous description fails to admit.
The fix is *tolerance*: the description must be loosened so that
the top can be either a block or a wedge. The figure completes the
trio of moves — enforce the supports, prevent the standing blocks
from touching, tolerate the shape of the top — that together yield
a workable uniframe for *arch*.
