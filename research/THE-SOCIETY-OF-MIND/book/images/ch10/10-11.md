# Figure 10-11 — Detour redrawn as a flat hierarchy

![figure](10-11.png)

**File:** `ch10/10-11.png`
**Appears in:** [../../som-10.9.md](../../som-10.9.md) — *Learning a hierarchy*

## What the image shows

A simplified tree with the same content as [10-10.md](10-10.md) but
with no room left for the old direct connections. The leaves
**EXTENT**, **DENSITY**, **ADD**, **REMOVE** feed into **SPATIAL** and
**CONFINED**, which feed into a single arrowhead labelled **MORE**.

## What it illustrates

What the detour looks like once it has been adopted and the old
wiring removed. The intermediate layer is now the only path from
leaves to MORE. The figure makes vivid Minsky's claim that the
strata visible in a mature mind are the fossilised record of
successive detours, each of which once ran beside an older route.
