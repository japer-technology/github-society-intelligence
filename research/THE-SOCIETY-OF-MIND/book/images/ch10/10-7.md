## What the image shows

A four-level inverted tree. At the top, **MORE** branches into
**APPEARANCE** and **HISTORY**. APPEARANCE branches into **SPATIAL**
and **NUMERICAL**; HISTORY branches into **REVERSIBLE** and
**CONFINED**. The leaves, from left to right, are **EXTENT**,
**DENSITY**, **COUNT**, **SPREAD**, **MATCH**, **COMPRESS**, **ADD**,
**REMOVE**.

## What it illustrates

A worked Society-of-More tuned for the eggs-and-cups problem. Spread
eggs make EXTENT large and DENSITY low — SPATIAL withdraws because
its children conflict, handing control to NUMERICAL (counting) or, if
counting is unavailable, to HISTORY, which asks whether eggs were
added, removed, or merely moved. The figure shows how a small
hierarchy of specialised judges can deliver the right answer without
any single agent that "knows" what *more* means.
