## What the image shows

Another four-level tree mirroring [10-7.md](10-7.md). MORE branches
into APPEARANCE and HISTORY; APPEARANCE branches into **SPATIAL** and
**QUANTITATIVE**; HISTORY branches into **REVERSIBLE** and **CONFINED**.
The leaves are **TALL**, **THIN**, **MEASURE**, **ESTIMATE**, **POUR**,
**SPOON**, **LEAK**, **SPILL**.

## What it illustrates

The same architecture adapted to liquids. The low-level agents now
include pouring, spooning, leaking, and spilling — domain-specific
ways to track changes in volume. Minsky's point is that the upper
layers are essentially reusable; what shifts between domains is the
leaf set. A growing child does not invent a new MORE for each
substance — it grows new leaves under stable administrators.
