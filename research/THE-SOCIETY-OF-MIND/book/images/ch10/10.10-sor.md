# Figure 10-10 — Implementation analysis (SOR / FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION)

**Source figure:** [10-10.md](10-10.md) — *A detour wrapped around the Society-of-More.*
**Analysis target:** [`FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/)
(plan-of-record for `.forgejo-society/` and
`.forgejo/workflows/forgejo-society.yaml`).

Figure 10-10 is [10-9](10-9.md) applied to a real hierarchy: the
mid-construction picture where some lines are dashed (older,
fading) and some are solid (newer, taking over). This analysis
records what the implementation does with the idea of *graceful,
mid-construction states*.

---

## The ideas Figure 10-10 actually carries

1. **Mid-construction is itself a legitimate state.** Not just
   *before* and *after* — there is a *during*.
2. **Old and new wiring coexist visibly.** The diagram explicitly
   draws both.
3. **Dashed lines record deprecation.** Older routes are marked,
   not deleted.
4. **The new intermediate nodes are addressable.** *SPATIAL* and
   *CONFINED* have names and positions in the same picture as the
   old lines.
5. **Leaves remain identical across the diagram.** *EXTENT*,
   *DENSITY*, *ADD*, *REMOVE* are unchanged.
6. **The apex remains identical.** *MORE* is at the top of both
   left and right halves.
7. **The figure refuses a clean cut-over.** It deliberately shows
   the messy middle.

---

## What the implementation already absorbs

### 1 — Branch-based mid-construction

A pull-request branch *is* a mid-construction state visible to
everyone. The plan's branch lifecycle in
[`07-policies-and-safety.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/07-policies-and-safety.md)
makes this normal.

### 2 — Old artefacts retained by Git

Git history retains both the old and the new structures across a
mid-construction edit. The figure's "draw both lines" property is
preserved at the version-control level.

### 3 — Mid-construction is visible in state

The per-run state directory in
[`08-state-and-memory.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/08-state-and-memory.md)
holds `blackboard.md`, `signals.jsonl`, and `diff-summary.md` —
artefacts that are by their nature partial and mid-process.

## What the implementation does not yet take into account

### A — No deprecation marker on agencies

Figure 10-10's dashed lines are *deprecation in place*. The plan has
no `deprecated_at:` or `superseded_by:` field on an agency manifest
in
[`05-agencies-critics-censors.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/05-agencies-critics-censors.md).
An agency is either listed and active or absent.

### B — No coexistence of two routes between the same endpoints

The figure shows *EXTENT → MORE* (dashed) and *EXTENT → SPATIAL →
MORE* (solid) simultaneously. The plan has no routing primitive at
all, so it cannot have two competing routes. The runtime pipeline
in
[`03-runtime-pipeline.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/03-runtime-pipeline.md)
fixes one phase chain per workflow.

### C — A "mid-construction" diagram has no first-class artefact

The plan can describe a target structure
([`01-target-layout.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/01-target-layout.md))
and a bootstrap checklist
([`10-bootstrap-checklist.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/10-bootstrap-checklist.md))
but does not have a `current-architecture.md` that records "this is
what is live, this is what is shadow, this is what is fading." The
figure's middle frame has no operational shape.

### D — Promotion is atomic in the plan

A new agency manifest, once committed, is active on the next run.
There is no smooth promotion arc in which the new layer's verdicts
*gradually* override the old layer's — the change is on/off at
merge time.

## Implication for the plan (no changes proposed here)

Figure 10-10 is the figure that says *honesty about middle states is
itself a design property*. The plan is honest about middle states
for *content* (branches, per-run state) but not for *agencies* (no
deprecation marker, no parallel routing, no live architecture
record, no gradual promotion). Any move to add a `deprecated_at:`
field, a parallel routing primitive, a `current-architecture.md`
artefact, or a gradual-promotion mechanism would touch
[`05-agencies-critics-censors.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/05-agencies-critics-censors.md),
[`03-runtime-pipeline.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/03-runtime-pipeline.md),
and
[`08-state-and-memory.md`](../../../../../FORGEJO-SOCIETY-IMPLEMENTATION/08-state-and-memory.md),
and so falls under the "stop and ask" rules in
[`AGENTS.md`](../../../../../AGENTS.md) §12 and
[`CLAUDE.md`](../../../../../CLAUDE.md).
