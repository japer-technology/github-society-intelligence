# Figure 10-10 — A detour wrapped around the Society-of-More

![figure](10-10.png)

**File:** `ch10/10-10.png`
**Appears in:** [../../som-10.9.md](../../som-10.9.md) — *Learning a hierarchy*

## What the image shows

Two diagrams. The left shows four leaf agents — **EXTENT**,
**DENSITY**, **ADD**, **REMOVE** — feeding directly into a single
**MORE** node. The right shows the same four leaves, but their
outputs are first routed through two new intermediate boxes,
**SPATIAL** and **CONFINED**, whose outputs then feed MORE; some of
the original direct lines are drawn dashed to mark them as the older,
now-bypassed connections.

## What it illustrates

The detour-building method of [10-9.md](10-9.md) applied to the
Society-of-More. The new administrators are inserted between the leaf
agents and the apex; once they are reliable, the direct lines from
leaves to MORE can be retired. The figure shows mid-construction —
old and new wiring coexist while the new layer earns its keep.
