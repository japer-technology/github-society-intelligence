# Figure 14-8 — One-way pipeline view of perception

![figure](14-8.png)

**File:** `ch14/14-8.png`
**Appears in:** [../../som-14.3.md](../../som-14.3.md) — *Seeing squares*

## What the image shows

A horizontal arrow-chain with five labelled boxes in sequence:
**World → Sensation → Perception → Recognition → Cognition.**

## What it illustrates

The naïve, strictly bottom-up picture of seeing — information
flows in only one direction, from the world through the senses up
into thought. The figure is presented in order to be rejected: it
cannot account for the way expectation shapes what is seen, and
[14-9.md](14-9.md) shows the correction.
